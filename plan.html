My first 100 days

A practical plan to stabilize operations, restore credibility, and get results fast.

1

Open the books

Publish a clear snapshot of budgets, contracts, projects, and priorities.

2

Set expectations for leadership

Standards for honesty, professionalism, and performance—with consequences.

3

Fix the basics

Immediate focus on the services residents feel every day: roads, responsiveness, and communication.

4

Resident-first communication

Consistent updates via website and social, with a simple way to track issues and timelines.

5

Build a results dashboard

Public metrics for projects, response times, spending, and commitments—updated regularly.
